About SarOak

🎯

Our Mission

SarOak is dedicated to preserving and promoting Myanmar literature in the digital age. We believe every reader deserves access to great books.

🌟

Our Vision

To become Myanmar's largest free digital library, connecting readers with literature that inspires, educates, and entertains.

📚

What We Offer

Free access to a curated collection of Myanmar ebooks across all genres — novels, poetry, history, self-help, and more.

💻

Open Source

SarOak is an open-source project. Contribute, suggest improvements, or submit books through our GitHub repository.

How to Contribute

1

Submit a Book

Have a Myanmar ebook you'd like to share? Contact us to add it to our collection.

2

Report Issues

Found a broken link or incorrect information? Let us know so we can fix it.

3

Spread the Word

Share SarOak with friends and fellow readers to help grow our community.
